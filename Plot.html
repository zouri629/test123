Summary of the main story (organized by myself and roughly translated.)

Seal
The seal of the Black Egg Temple is protected by three dreamers:Lurien, Herrah, and Monomon,and
what was sealed by them was the "plague."
Plague
In fact, the plague is the god of light,The Radiance, in order not to be forgotten, the creation
under her wrath.she enters the dream of bugs and makes them out of control and become aggressive.
The Radiance
The reason why it will be forgotten by the people is because the founder of the kingdom, Wyrm,
taught the people a lot.The attempt to make them forget The Radiance led to Hollownest , which
had flourished, also because of his gradual decline under the plague.
Wyrm
The Pale King's(Wyrm) response is to join forces with Void to seal The Radiance in an container,
hollow knight,and seal it with Dreamers guarding it.
Container
A child of the pale king and the white lady, a hollow creature in a mask.Containers can't have
feelings and emotions, but the Pale King's relationship with Hollow knight makes it feel emotions,
so the seal gradually weakens.
Dreamers
Wyrm makes a deal with Herrah,who must guard the seal,and Wyrm promises to give her a child.Hornet
is the creation of Wyrm and Herrah's deal.
Monomon anticipates the arrival of the protagonist and asks Quirrel to help. unseal her seal.Quirrel
will lose a lot of power in this incident.
Lurien is one of the least well-documented.
White lady
